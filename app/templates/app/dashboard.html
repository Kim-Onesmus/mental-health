About This Project

Mental Health Facility Mapping Initiative

This platform is designed to map, document, and connect mental health organizations and facilities across the region. It helps improve visibility, collaboration, and access to mental health services.

Organizations can submit their facilities for review. Once approved, they become publicly visible and accessible to partners, stakeholders, and communities seeking mental health support services.

All facility submissions undergo review by the IDL team before approval to ensure accuracy and quality of information.
